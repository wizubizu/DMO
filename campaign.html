CAMPAIGN ACTIVITIES



Apart from being a visionary young man, one of the reasons Dr Olugbile is widely loved and accepted by the people of his constituency (including the traditional and religious leaders) is his people-oriented development interventions which he does with full community participation and ownership.

Some of these interventions include;

  • Employment of contract teachers to bridge the gap in number of teachers at two secondary schools.
  • Setting up of free extramural classes across the constituency with monthly allowance being provided for the teachers.
  • Setting up of free outdoor viewing centres for 2014 World Cup in four communities in the constituency.
  • Repair of damaged borehole in Okuku.
  • Football Talent Hunt with Emmanuel Babayaro for football players in Odo-Otin/Ifelodun/Boripe federal constituency. A total of 24 players were selected and currently being prepared to ply their trade abroad.

Other activities being planned for the campaign include;

  • Free medical and surgical outreaches including ophthalmological and dental care.
  • Free computer training to enhance the capacity of the youth in the constituency.
  • Agricultural intervention for farmers such as distribution of fertilizers and farming tools.
  • Women empowerment and poverty reduction programs through setting up a cooperative society in each ward and providing them with a take –off microcredit grant.

THE APPEAL

Dr Olugbile deserves the opportunity to demonstrate greater service to Nigeria at the House of Representatives. A support for his candidature is a support for positive change and a better tomorrow for our great nation.
